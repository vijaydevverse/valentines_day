--❤ Forever and Always

In the past, he lived alone, enjoying solitude while longing for love.

A lonely corridor

A lonely mountain ridge

A lonely subway ride

A lonely amusement park

But he stayed optimistic, smiling and waiting

Life inevitably has storms

He always managed to face them calmly

With a bright and confident smile

Life is not always smooth

But every time, he faced it bravely
Always ready for life’s challenges

But where is his love?

In the mirror? He couldn’t believe it

He asked the tree, “Where is my love?”
The tree said: maybe not far away

So he kept walking alone
Across the endless snow

Until one day, he met her

He liked her. Because of her, his smile became brighter.

But would she like him?

One day, he finally gathered his courage

He was so happy

Every day, he went to see her

Then the two of them went out together

At night, he would take her home

Only late at night
Did he return home alone

Then he happily fell asleep, hoping to dream of her

He soon became her shopping assistant

They went to many places together

He also became a foodie like her

Later, they moved in together

So he began learning new skills

Cooking

Every morning, they ate the food they made themselves

Then happily went to work together from the same station

He felt so happy
Because he had a home
A place with her

Sometimes, they would argue

He didn’t want this

It must be something I did wrong, he thought

Without her by his side, the view meant nothing

Without her behind him

How could he ever fly higher and farther?

He wanted to change for her
So he made a wish

I am willing to change for you

Love is like flowers and grass
It needs tolerance to grow

Later, her birthday was coming
He began preparing a gift

Watching the gifts get ready one by one
He was very happy
Because he imagined
Her joyful smile

Thinking about the lifetime ahead
He was very happy
Because he could travel
All over the world with her

He will continue to work hard
For the castle of their dreams

One day, she said she was afraid to ride roller coasters. So he took her somewhere else. It was a small moment in their happy life, but it belonged only to them.

She: How far is forever?
He: One second longer than time—that is forever.
She: How big is the world?
He: Wherever you go, that is how big the world is.

To find one heart and never be apart

Happy Valentine’s Day

I love you

Their story will continue forever.
Whether exciting or ordinary, it will all be love.
